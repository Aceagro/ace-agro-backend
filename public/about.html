About Ace Agro Tech

Founded in 2023, ACE Agro Tech is a dynamic agri-business headquartered in Burdwan, West Bengal, India, committed to revolutionizing sustainable agriculture and global trade. We specialize in three key areas: an online nursery for premium plants, organic farming of high-quality produce, and the export of superior Miniket rice sourced from the fertile regions of Burdwan and Hooghly districts.

Our Expertise

Online Nursery: Through our intuitive e-commerce platform, we provide a diverse selection of plants, including fruit trees, ornamental flowers, and herbs. Each plant is nurtured with care, ensuring healthy delivery to gardening enthusiasts and landscapers worldwide.

Organic Farming: We champion eco-friendly farming, growing organic vegetables, fruits, and grains without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. Our farms adhere to strict organic standards, delivering fresh, chemical-free produce for health-conscious consumers.

Rice Export: We export premium Miniket rice, a sought-after non-basmati variety known for its slender grains, subtle aroma, and fluffy texture. Sourced from trusted farmers in Burdwan and Hooghly, our rice is meticulously processed, sortexed, and packed to meet global standards, catering to markets in Africa, South East Asia, and the Middle East.

Why Choose ACE Agro Tech?

Rooted in West Bengal’s rich agricultural legacy, we blend traditional expertise with modern innovation to ensure quality and sustainability. Our Miniket rice boasts a moisture content of max 14% and less than 2% broken grains, guaranteeing export-ready excellence. We ship globally from Kolkata Sea Port within 7 days, offering flexible packaging in 10kg, 25kg, or 50kg bags.

At ACE Agro Tech, we are dedicated to empowering farmers, promoting green living, and delivering Bengal’s finest to the world. Join us in our mission to grow, nourish, and trade sustainably!
